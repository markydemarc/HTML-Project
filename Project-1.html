Mr.Muscle - Don't Help Me

Don't Help Me emphasizes a very popular phrase in male gender: I help at home. A phrase that seems like something good, but when we delve deeper into what it means it speaks of an inequality in the responsibilities in household chores distribution. The objective is to support an initiative that promotes equitable behavior between genders and encourage them to share household chores.
